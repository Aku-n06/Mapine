Privacy Policy for Mapine

Last Updated: 19 November 2023

1. Data Collection and Storage:

Mapine allows users to manually save favorite locations on the map. This includes GPS information, an emoji, a name, and a description. All data is stored locally on the user's device, and [Company Name] does not collect or retain any user data since we do not have any servers or networking involved.

2. Data Usage:

The collected data solely serves the purpose of displaying favorites on the map within the app. No user data is used for any other purposes.

3. Third-Party Involvement:

Mapine does not utilize any third-party frameworks, analytics tools, or external services. The app operates independently on the user's device.

4. Data Security:

Mapine does not implement encryption or authentication measures as all data is stored locally on the user's device. Users have the option to manually export or import data to/from a file.

5. Data Sharing:

Currently, users cannot share data directly. However, future updates may include a feature allowing users to share lists of favorites with friends, and this functionality will be managed through Apple iCloud.

6. Collection of Additional Data:

Mapine does not collect data about the places users add to their favorites.

7. Privacy Policy Updates:

Updates to this Privacy Policy will be reflected in the content of this page. Users are encouraged to check the Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.

Contact Information:

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact mapine@proton.me.

By using Mapine, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
